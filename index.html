For Creators

Can I choose the creators myself?

Yes. You can either select creators manually or let our team and smart system recommend the best match.

How do I know if the campaign is working?

We provide real-time analytics and detailed reports showing reach, engagement, conversions, and ROI metrics for your campaigns.

Do I have to negotiate rates with each creator?

No, we offer wholesale rates that are pre-negotiated. You get transparent, fixed pricing without the hassle of individual negotiations.

Can I manage multiple client campaigns?

Yes, our agency dashboard allows you to manage multiple client campaigns simultaneously with separate billing and reporting.

Do you offer white-label solutions?

Yes, we provide white-label solutions for agencies wanting to offer influencer marketing services under their own brand.

What reporting features are available?

Comprehensive reporting including campaign performance, ROI analysis, audience insights, and customizable client reports.

How do I get paid for collaborations?

Payments are processed automatically after content approval. We support multiple payment methods and ensure timely payments.

What are the content requirements?

Content requirements vary by campaign but typically include high-quality visuals, authentic messaging, and adherence to brand guidelines.

How do I join the platform?

Simply sign up with your social media profiles. If you have 1,000+ followers, you can start receiving collaboration opportunities immediately.
